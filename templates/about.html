About Page

Electrical / Electronics Engineering (EEE), deals with the engineering problems, opportunities, and needs of electrical, electronics, computer, telecommunication systems and related industries. This branch provides students with a wide range of fundamental knowledge in core disciplines such as communications, control systems, signal processing, radio frequency design, microprocessors, microelectronics, power generation, and electrical machines. The discipline focuses on design and manufacture of electrical, electronic devised, computers and their component parts, as well as on the integration of components into complex systems.

Electrical Electronics Engineering started at the University of Jos in 2014/2015 academic calendar along with three other departments, Civil, Mechanical and Mining under the faculty of Engineering.

The philosophy of the department is to “impart Electrical and Electronics Engineering knowledge and skills at all levels to men and women of all race, religious or political beliefs in order to serve the needs of the nation and mankind inn general”.
